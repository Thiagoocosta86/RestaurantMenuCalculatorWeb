About Us

Dublin Restaurant was established in the early 80s, it was founded by Jim O'Donnel, a food lover and a great Chef. Nowadays the restaurant is ran by Jim's sons. George and Tomas, both have their father same passion, and both are Chefs. George runs the overall menu and Tom is specialized in vegetarian cousine.
